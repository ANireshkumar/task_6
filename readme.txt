##How to Play
1.Click on a card to flip it and reveal its value.
2.Try to find a matching pair by flipping over another card.
3.If the cards match, they will remain face-up.
4.If the cards do not match, they will flip back after a short delay.
5.Continue flipping cards until all pairs are found.
6.Use the "Restart" button to reset the game at any time.

## Technologies Used
- HTML for the structure of the game.
- CSS for styling and layout.
- JavaScript for game logic and DOM manipulation.
